# Lumora

Your daily dose of cosmic clarity — inspirational quotes, astrology, and moon guidance to make sense of what the universe is doing to you today.

## What it does

People wake up feeling off, anxious, or like things aren't going their way — and have no framework to explain it. Lumora gives spiritually curious users a daily touchpoint: AI-generated inspirational quotes delivered in-app and by email, plus astrology and moon phase guidance that answers practical questions like "is Mercury retrograde affecting me?" or "should I cut my hair today?" Built for the person who checks Co-Star but wants something more focused, more uplifting, and more actionable.
